CASE 001

Bulk customer-data recovery across 12 distributed sites

Situation

An automotive group's nightly customer-data sync had died. Records stopped flowing between the CRM and the dealerships. Support tickets piled up; nobody had a count of what was missing.

Approach

Built a reconciliation engine that pulled the three sources (CSV export, REST JSON, legacy dump), matched records by ID, and flagged every field-level conflict with proof of what each side held.

Result

Recovered 1,830 records across 12 sites with a 100% match rate on record ID, zero destructive writes, and the nightly batch back to 0 errors.

CASE 002

Real-time gateway stall on a streaming-only upstream

Situation

A client gateway had gone silent. Dashboards showed no live data, but the upstream was up and serving.

Approach

Read the upstream contract instead of guessing. It only speaks Server-Sent Events; the client was requesting a non-streaming response and timing out waiting for a body that never comes.

Result

Restored streaming with a one-line client change. No sudo, no network change, no downtime.
